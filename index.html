Narges Mehran

About me

  • A doctoral candidate in distributed and parallel systems group at Alpen-Adria University (of Klagenfurt), Austria;
  • From Jan. 2021 until Dec. 2023, I was a research assistant in the EU DataCloud project.
  • From Mar. 2018 until Nov. 2022, I was a teaching and research assistant at Alpen-Adria University (of Klagenfurt), Austria.
